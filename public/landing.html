Setup — Day 1
1
You sign up

Purchase through Whop and fill out a short onboarding form — company name, your phone number, and your booking link if you have one.

2
We configure Reva for your business

We set up a dedicated phone number for your company and train Reva with your business name, service area, and availability. Takes us about an hour.

3
You forward your calls (takes 2 minutes)

We send you a simple guide to forward your existing business number to Reva's line. One setting on your phone — done. You can turn it off anytime.

4
We test everything live

We call your number, walk through a full conversation, confirm the alerts and texts are firing correctly, and make sure it all sounds natural before going live.

Every Day After That
5
Customer calls your number

Reva picks up instantly — 24/7, no hold music, no voicemail. She greets them naturally, asks about their roof, and keeps the conversation going.

6
Reva qualifies the lead

She collects their name, address, what's wrong, whether it's a home or business, urgency level, and the best time for a free estimate — all in a natural conversation.

7
You get an instant alert

The moment the call ends, you get a text with everything: name, address, issue, urgency, and appointment time. No calls to listen back to.

8
Customer gets a summary text

Reva texts the customer their appointment details right after the call. If they don't respond to the initial text, Reva follows up automatically over the next week.

9
You close the job

Show up to the estimate. The lead is already qualified, they're expecting you, and Reva already built the rapport. You just close it.
